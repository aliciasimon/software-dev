Meet the Members

A seven member South Korean boy group under BigHit Entertainment that debuted June 13, 2013 on MCOUNTDOWN with the song “No More Dream” followed by “We Are Bulletproof Pt. 2”

Member Updates

    The Leader and Main Rapper of the band BTS, was apart of the original line-up in 2010 before debuting in 2013.

    The Lead Rapper of the band BTS, also known as August D. Is extremely versatile being able to do songwriting, producing, rapping, etc.

    Main Vocalist/Lead Dancer/Maknae
